LIGHT CITY SMP — Official Tournament Rulebook

Prize Pool: $70.00 | By registering, you acknowledge and agree to every rule listed in this document.

1. General Conduct & SMP Rules

  • All standard Light City SMP server rules are in full effect throughout the entire tournament.
  • Disruptive behavior, trolling, intentional griefing, or refusing to follow admin orders will result in immediate removal.
  • Any form of harassment or targeted toxicity is forbidden.
  • By signing up, you formally agree to all rules listed here.

2. Equipment & Inventory Requirements

  • Participants must compete using their own independently acquired in-game gear.
  • A mandatory inventory check will be conducted prior to entering the tournament arena. Players deemed under-geared by administration will not be permitted to participate.
  • The use of a Mace is permitted, but it is strictly limited to one per participant. Only the designated holder is allowed to utilize the Mace.
  • Utility items, including TNT and TNT minecarts, are permitted for use during combat unless specifically listed on the server's banned items registry.
