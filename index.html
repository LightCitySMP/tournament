Tournament Rulebook

General Conduct
01All standard Light City SMP server rules are in full effect throughout the entire tournament.
02Disruptive behavior, trolling, intentional griefing, or refusing to follow admin orders will result in immediate removal.
03Any form of harassment or targeted toxicity is strictly forbidden.
04By participating, you formally agree to every rule listed in this document.
Equipment & Inventory
05Participants must compete using their own independently acquired in-game gear.
06A mandatory inventory check will be conducted prior to entering the arena. Under-geared players may not be permitted to participate.
07The Mace is permitted but strictly limited to one per participant — only the designated holder may use it.
08Utility items including TNT and TNT Minecarts are permitted unless listed on the server's banned items registry.
2v2 Format
09Teams consist of exactly 2 players. Team compositions are locked at registration.
10Matches are Best of 3. Grand Finals are Best of 5.
11Both players must be present within 5 minutes of match time or the team forfeits.
12Admin rulings on disputes are final and non-negotiable.
Anti-Cheat & PC Check
13All participants consent to a PC check by owner and admins to verify no exploits or hack clients are present.
14Any player found using a banned client, macro, or exploit will be immediately disqualified and banned from future events.
15Suggested minimum tier is lt3+ on any recognized tierlist. Include the tierlist name when registering.
Prizes & Payouts
16The total prize pool is $70.00 USD. Distribution will be announced before the tournament begins.
17Prizes are paid out within 48 hours of the final match. Disqualified teams forfeit any prize claim.
18Administration reserves the right to withhold or redistribute prizes in the event of confirmed rule violations.
